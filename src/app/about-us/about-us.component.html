About Us

Erin Moynihan

Erin is one of Camp Half-Pod’s co-hosts, a daughter of Athena who loves a good angst moment. She runs Camp Half-Pod’s social media, and occasionally edits episodes. Erin is a writer, her debut novel Laurel Everywhere was published in 2020. Outside of writing and podcasting, she works for a university.

Manasa Acharya

Manasa is one of Camp Half-Pod’s co-hosts, a daughter of Persephone with a love for villain monologues and chaotic characters. Manasa is our Greek myth expert, and loves to dive into the weird and wacky parts that Uncle Rick leaves out. Outside of podcasting, she is finishing up grad school.

Sammie Moynihan

Sammie edits Camp-Half Pod episodes. A child of Poseidon, she’s a film student who loves to animate and also is a fantastic sister for agreeing to do this for Erin.
